Reward Points with Spex4Less

Earn points for every purchase and get discounts on future orders. Find out how our reward system works!

How to Earn Reward Points

You can earn reward points in multiple ways:

  • Earn points every time you make a purchase—get 1 point for every £1 spent.
  • Refer a friend, and you will earn 1,100 points (£11 equivalent) once they make their first purchase.
  • Earn points by engaging with our brand through promotions and special offers throughout the year.

How Much Are Reward Points Worth?

Each reward point is worth £0.01. For example, 100 points will give you £1 off your next purchase. Start accumulating points now for even bigger savings!

Where to Find Your Reward Points

To check your reward points balance, simply log in to your account. You can view your total available points in the "My Rewards" section under your account dashboard.

How to Use Your Reward Points

You can use your reward points at checkout. Once you have enough points, you will have the option to redeem them for a discount on your order. Just select the "Apply Reward Points" option when reviewing your cart.
