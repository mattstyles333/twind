About Us

Established in 2004, Spex4less is one of the first online glasses companies in the industry and provides prescription glasses, sunglasses and sports glasses at a fraction of high-street prices. With deep roots within optics we have successfully mastered the supply of high-quality, yet affordable spectacles via the internet, with the highest standard of care and service.

Based in Hoylake, Wirral, United Kingdom, our's is a small family-run business whose ethos is focused on ensuring that every customer receives only the best from us. We pride ourselves on our amazing "Excellent" Trustpilot rating from over 25,000 reviews. Our business comprises a huge amount of repeat customers, which speaks volumes about the story of our success.

Our prescription glasses are made here in our on-site state-of-the-art lab, and our delivery time for most orders is within 1 to 7 days. All queries are dealt with efficiently by our helpful customer service team who are always on hand to take your call and answer any questions you may have.

What We Offer

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

At Spex4Less, buying glasses online is risk-free with our 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not thrilled with your new glasses for any reason, return them for a full refund or exchange. Plus, enjoy a free 12-month frame warranty. Click here to learn more.

High-Quality Frames

We offer frames from the same suppliers as high street opticians, including 50 premium designer brands and thousands of fashionable options. Our specialty lies in varifocal glasses with personalized optical measurements. Customers also love our prescription sunglasses and sports glasses, catering to every style and budget.

Our Lab

All glazing is conducted on-site by our team of highly skilled lab technicians. Our glazing laboratory was recently rebuilt and installed with brand-new, state-of-the-art technology. With a pair of new robots from the world-renowned Nidek Corporation, we are able to ensure superior, more consistent glazing than ever before. The high standard of lens finishing is extremely well received by all our customers.

Lenses

Our lens manufacturers supply the majority of opticians you will find on the high street, and there is absolutely no compromise on quality. For the sake of transparency, we use the following lens suppliers whose service and quality remain consistently high:- Essilor, Zeiss, Hoya, Jai Kudo, WLC, Spectrum and Shamir.

Quality Control

Every pair of glasses we produce goes through a thorough quality control process. We meticulously examine frame color, size, condition, and alignment, as well as lens condition, prescription accuracy, tint color, tint depth, and UV transmittance. Only after passing these checks are the glasses sent for dispatch, ensuring that you receive the highest quality eyewear.
