Why Choose Reglazing?

Reglazing is the process of replacing the lenses in your existing frames. It's an eco-friendly choice that allows you to keep the style you love while updating your prescription or lens type. By choosing reglazing, you reduce waste and contribute to a more sustainable future.

Our expert technicians ensure a perfect fit and finish, giving your glasses a new lease on life without the need for new frames.

Who Our Reglaze Service Is For

  • Updating Your Prescription

    Our reglazing service lets you keep the style you trust, with lenses that reflect your most up-to-date prescription.

  • Environmentally Conscious

  • Seeking an Upgrade

All Your Reglazing Queries. Answered.

Get answers to common questions about the reglazing process.

How long does it take to receive a reglaze?

Once we receive your frames, it usually takes between 1 to 14 days to send back your reglazed glasses.

Are there any frame conditions unsuitable for reglazing?

We can reglaze almost any frame. If you're unsure whether your frames are suitable, please contact our optical service team at 0151 303 0066.

Can coatings be added during the reglaze process?

Yes, you can enhance your lenses with special coatings during the reglazing process. Options include anti-reflective, scratch-resistant, blue light protection, and honeycomb coatings to suit your lifestyle and vision needs.

What does reglazing mean?

Reglazing involves replacing the lenses in your current glasses frame with new ones. This is ideal if your prescription has changed, your lenses are damaged, or you want to upgrade to different lens types, such as anti-glare or transition lenses, while keeping your existing frame.

Can I reglaze a frame not purchased from JP Opticians?

Yes, you can reglaze frames not bought from JP Opticians. We offer reglazing services for most frame types, regardless of where they were purchased. Send us your frame, and we'll fit it with new lenses according to your prescription and preferences.

Will you return my original lenses and case?

Yes, we will return your original case along with your reglazed glasses.
