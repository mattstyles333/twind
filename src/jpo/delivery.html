Delivery Information

At JP Opticians, we're committed to getting your eyewear to you as quickly and smoothly as possible.

Production Times

At JP Opticians, every order is custom made to your exact specifications. We take pride in crafting each pair of glasses with precision and care.

Orders are typically dispatched within 7-14 working days from the date of order. This timeframe allows us to ensure the highest quality and accuracy in your custom eyewear.

Shipping Methods

At JP Opticians, we are dedicated to delivering your orders swiftly and securely through our partnership with Royal Mail. Throughout the shipping process, you will receive:

  • Frequent email updates on your order status
  • A tracking number for real-time delivery updates
  • Flexibility to adjust delivery dates or specify a safe location for drop-off
  • Expected delivery time on the day of delivery
  • Delivery confirmation with photo and GPS coordinates of the delivery location
