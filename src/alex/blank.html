What Are Varifocals?

Varifocal lenses, also known as progressive lenses, are a revolutionary solution that combines multiple prescriptions in a single lens. They provide seamless vision correction for all distances, eliminating the need for multiple pairs of glasses.

How Varifocals Work

Distance Vision

Upper portion of the lens for viewing far objects

Intermediate Vision

Middle area perfect for computer work

Near Vision

Lower portion optimized for reading

Key Benefits

Seamless Vision

Enjoy smooth transitions between all viewing distances without visible lines

Modern Design

Sleek and contemporary appearance without visible segmentation
