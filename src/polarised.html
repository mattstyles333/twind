Polarised Sunglasses

Why Consider Polarised Lenses?

Many people who have tried polarised sunglasses find that you may never go back to standard sunglasses. When you're driving, the surface glare off the road and other cars can give you eye strain and make you drowsy. However, polarised lenses will block the glare making you feel more alert so you can pay more attention to your surroundings.

Polarised sports sunglasses

Polarized sunglasses can also contribute to improved sporting performances. This is attributed to athletes being able to achieve clearer vision, enabling quicker reactions. Sports polarized frames typically feature a base curve design (wrap-around frame).
