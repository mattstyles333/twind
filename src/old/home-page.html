How to choose your glasses

Choosing your glasses has never been easier, simply follow these 4 easy steps!

Step 1 - Choose Your Frame

At Spex4Less, we offer a diverse range of frames to suit your personal preferences. Whatever you're looking for, we've got you covered. Our collection includes both affordable stock frames and impressive designer options. Find your perfect fit today!

Step 2 - Choose Your Lenses

The next Step is to choose from our selection of lenses. When selecting lenses you will be given an option of customising your lenses with our array of coatings.

Step 3 - Enter Your Prescription

The next step is to send your prescription to us. If you are waiting on a prescription but still want to order, that's fine. You can send your prescription at a later date to info@spex4less.com

Step 4 - Order

Once you have made your order you’ll receive a confirmation email. If you have any further questions regarding # your order don't hesitate to get in touch with our customer service team.
