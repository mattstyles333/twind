Our Lab

All glazing is conducted on-site by our team of highly skilled lab technicians. Our glazing laboratory was recently rebuilt and installed with brand-new, state-of-the-art technology.

With a pair of new robots from the world-renowned Nidek Corporation, we are able to ensure superior, more consistent glazing than ever before. The high standard of lens finishing is extremely well received by all our customers.

Our lens manufacturers supply the majority of opticians you will find on the high street, and there is absolutely no compromise on quality. For the sake of transparency, we use the following lens suppliers whose service and quality remain consistently high:- Essilor, Zeiss, Hoya, Jai Kudo, WLC, Spectrum and Shamir.
