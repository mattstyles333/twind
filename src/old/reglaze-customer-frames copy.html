About our reglaze service

Our reglaze service allows you to update your current frames with a new prescription, providing a cost-effective and eco-friendly alternative to discarding them. Ideal for those who love their existing frames, this service can accommodate your latest prescription or replace damaged lenses. Additionally, we offer premium upgrades like coatings, lens thinning, or varifocals to enhance your eyewear experience. If you prefer, we can also use the prescription details from your current frames.

How to reglaze your glasses

Step 1
Select Your Lenses
Start by selecting the type of frame you're reglazing, be it fully rimmed, semi-rimless, rimless, or wraparound. Once you've made your choice, explore our extensive lens options to customise your lenses to your preference.
Step 2
Add Your Prescription
For the next step, please forward your prescription to us. If you haven't received your prescription yet but are eager to place an order, that's okay. Initiate your order now, and when you do get your prescription, just send it over to us at [email protected].
Step 3
Order and Post
After you've placed your order, please send your glasses along with your order number to Spex4Less at Everite House, Carr Lane, Hoylake, Wirral, CH47 4BG. As soon as we've processed your order, we'll provide you with an update, informing you of the expected timeframe.
Step 4
Receive your reglaze
Get excited about receiving your upgraded glasses in the mail, bringing enhanced vision right to your doorstep. As you unbox your fresh and improved eyewear, prepare to appreciate the world around you with newfound clarity and comfort, making every detail come to life in vibrant, crystal-clear focus.
