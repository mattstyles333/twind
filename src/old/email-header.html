Finding Your Perfect Glasses: A Style Guide

Feeling overwhelmed by endless options? At Spex4less, we have over 10,000+ frames to choose from and we understand picking the perfect pair can feel daunting. But don't worry, we've got you covered! We have a fool proof method to help you navigate the sea of options and find the ideal frames for your unique needs and preferences. Keep reading to discover how to make choosing your glasses a breeze!

Size

Make sure to pay attention to the size of your glasses to ensure they fit comfortably. The width of the frame should match the width of your face to avoid any slipping mishaps. If you already have a pair of glasses, check the measurements on the inside of the arms.
