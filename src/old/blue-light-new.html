What Are Blue Light Glasses?

Blue light glasses, also known as blue light blocking glasses, are specially designed eyewear that helps protect your eyes from the potential negative effects of prolonged exposure to blue light emitted by digital screens and artificial lighting.

Specialty Coating

With specialized lenses featuring a blue light coating, these glasses selectively reduce the amount of blue light that reaches your eyes.

Reducing eye-strain and enhanding Comfort

Their specialized coatings subtly minimize discomfort and potential sleep disturbances, subtly elevating the digital experience.

What Are The Effects of Blue Light on Your Eyes?

In today's digital-centric world, we're constantly exposed to screens emitting blue light which can have significant effects on our eye health. Prolonged screen time can result in digital eye strain, causing discomfort, dryness, and headaches. Moreover, the blue light's interference with our natural sleep patterns, primarily during evening hours, disrupts the production of melatonin, potentially leading to sleep disturbances.

New research has raised concerns about the possibility of blue light causing damage to the retina when we're exposed to it for a long time. This highlights the importance of taking steps to reduce these potential effects before they become a problem.

To combat these issues, blue light glasses have emerged as a practical solution, offering lenses equipped with blue light filtering coatings to help mitigate the impact of prolonged screen exposure.
