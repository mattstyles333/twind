Lens Coatings

Selecting the perfect frames for your glasses can be a challenge in itself, and then there's the added decision of choosing the right lens coatings. Lens coatings are a special treatment designed to offer extra protection for your lenses. They not only enhance your vision but also elevate the overall quality of your new glasses. These coatings work wonders by boosting durability, improving performance, and enhancing the aesthetics of all lens types. The coating you ultimately pick should align with your unique visual requirements and lifestyle. Delve into our informative guide to discover the distinct benefits of each lens coating, empowering you to make the ideal choice that perfectly suits your needs.

Different Coatings

Discover an array of lens coatings that not only enhance your vision but also elevate your style. Our range of coatings offer various benefits tailored to your prescription.

Anti-Reflective Coating

By eliminating surface reflections on your lenses, it allows more light to pass through, enhancing vision and reducing eye strain. Plus, it gives your glasses a sleeker appearance. This coating can be especially advantageous for drivers, as it enhances safety by swiftly recognizing potential road hazards. And at night, those annoying reflections from street lamps or headlights are significantly diminished, making your drive more comfortable.

Blue Light Coating

Blue light, found in both natural daylight and electronic devices like computers, smartphones, TVs, and strip lights, boasts a short wavelength that easily infiltrates the eyes. Yet, overexposure to this light can lead to retinal damage, eye strain, and fatigue. By incorporating a Blue light filter into your glasses lenses, this light is neutralised, preventing its intrusion into your eyes. The result? A more soothing visual experience, less strain, and diminished risk of eye damage all from blue light glasses

UV and Durable+ Coating

Ultraviolet protecting coatings can be applied to clear glasses as well as sunglasses. This is especially important for people who spend a lot of time outside. Even on cloudy days, the UV risk can still be high. A UV coating will block harmful rays and keep your eyes protected against potential damage.

Anti-Fogging Coating

Our anti-fog coating works like a charm by absorbing moisture from the warm air on your lenses, effectively preventing those pesky fog-ups. This special treatment proves especially handy when you're sporting a mask, sipping a hot beverage, cooking, or engaging in outdoor pursuits. Say goodbye to cloudy lenses and hello to crystal-clear visibility even in challenging conditions.

Honeycomb Coating

Honeycomb Coating A honeycomb lens is designed to physically redirect and distribute excessive light or glare from entering your eyes, from any direction. Honeycomb technology is an advanced anti-reflective coating, particularly useful for night driving, as it improves vision accuracy and reduces eye fatigue.

Photochromic Coating

Transition lenses rely on specialised photochromic technology to darken when exposed to UV light and then lighten as the light source diminishes. These lenses contain special photochromic molecules that react to the presence of UV radiation from the sun, causing the lenses to darken. As the amount of UV light decreases, the lenses gradually become lighter again, providing optimal visual clarity in any lighting conditions.
