Founded in 2004, Spex4less stands as a pioneering online eyewear retailer, offering prescription glasses, sunglasses, and sports eyewear at prices significantly lower than high-street. About Spex4Less

Headquartered in the Northwest of England, just outside of Liverpool in a small town called Hoylake. We are a close-knit, family-owned glasses business focused around the customer and driven by a commitment to excellence. This commitment is mirrored in our stellar 4.9 rating on Trustpilot, backed by over 27,000 reviews.

Precision Craftsmanship

Every pair of prescription glasses is crafted in our state of the art on-site laboratory, ensuring a swift delivery time, typically between 1 to 10 working days. Should you have any inquiries, our responsive customer service team is always available, ensuring every question is answered with utmost care and efficiency.
