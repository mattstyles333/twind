Learn more about us here at Spex4Less

Founded in 2004, Spex4less stands as a pioneering online eyewear retailer, offering prescription glasses, sunglasses, and sports eyewear at prices significantly lower than high-street counterparts. Our deep expertise in optics enables us to deliver top-notch yet affordable eyewear online, underpinned by unparalleled care and service.

Customer Service

Situated in Hoylake, Wirral, UK, we are a close-knit, family-owned enterprise driven by a commitment to excellence. This commitment is mirrored in our stellar "Excellent" rating on Trustpilot, backed by over 22,000 reviews.

Every pair of prescription glasses is crafted in our advanced on-site laboratory, ensuring a swift delivery time, typically between 1 to 7 days. Should you have any inquiries, our responsive customer service team is always available, ensuring every question is answered with utmost care and efficiency.

At Spex4less, we blend fashion with clarity. Our experienced customer service team is on hand for all your optical queries and any details related to your order. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our #1 rating on Trustpilot. To get in touch, use our live chat or call us directly on 0151 632 6611.

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

Purchasing glasses online from Spex4Less is completely risk-free. Every order you place comes with our 100% no-quibble satisfaction guarantee. This means that for any reason at all you are not totally thrilled with your new glasses, you can return them for a full refund or exchange. Furthermore, because we want your new glasses to serve you well, we also include a free 12 months frame warranty. Click here to read more.

Frames & Lenses

All our frames are sourced from the exact same suppliers as regular high street opticians. We have over 50 premium designer brands at your disposal, and thousands of fashionable frames to choose from. Varifocal glasses are a specialty of ours, using our own proven system for determining individual optical measurements. Prescription sunglasses and sports glasses are also very popular among our customers. There is something to suit every style and budget.
