A closer look at the technology and principles behind sunglasses, mirrors, and tints.

Understanding Light and Design

Sunglasses, mirrors, and tints are more than just fashion. They blend science with style, helping protect our eyes and enhance aesthetics. Our guide delves into the simple magic behind these everyday items. Dive in to discover their importance and innovation.

Tints, Mirrors, and Photochromics

Sunglass tints not only provide a stylistic touch but also filter specific wavelengths of light, enhancing visual clarity and significantly reducing the harshness of glare, making it easier on the eyes.

Mirrored lenses come with a reflective optical coating on the outside, which helps to reflect sunlight away from the eyes, offering a cooler and more comfortable view, especially in intensely bright conditions.

Photochromic lenses are designed with innovative technology that allows them to adjust in real-time, darkening when exposed to sunlight and becoming clear once again when indoors, providing versatile vision protection.

Sunglass Tints
