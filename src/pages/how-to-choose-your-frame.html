How To Choose Your Glasses

Choosing your glasses has never been easier! The following 5 steps will help you find the perfect frame.

Step 1
Size
At Spex4less, the fit is crucial. Ensure the frame's width aligns with your face to prevent slippage or discomfort. If you have fitting glasses, check the measurements inside the arms to guide your next choice. A well-fitted pair can elevate comfort and style simultaneously.
Step 2
Face Shape
Your glasses should accentuate your unique style and face shape. Whether round, square, oval, or heart-shaped, Spex4less offers frames that elevate your look and boost your confidence. Let us help you find that perfect silhouette that speaks to your essence here.
Step 3
Colour
Seeking versatility? Dark shades like black or brown are timeless. Feeling vibrant? Dive into hues like red or green. Spex4less provides a color palette for every personality, ensuring you find your perfect match. From muted to vivid, our collection caters to all moods.
Step 4
Brands
Brands can influence design and quality, but it's a choice, not a mandate. Spex4less showcases both affordable and designer frames, all curated to meet diverse styles and preferences. Whether you're brand-conscious or value-driven, we have something tailored for you.
Step 5
Lenses
Lastly, lens selection is pivotal. It may seem daunting, but with Spex4less, it's a breeze. From single vision to varifocals, our range caters to varied needs. Our dedicated team is always on hand to guide you through the selection process, ensuring clarity and comfort in every pair you choose.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When purchasing a new pair of glasses, the easiest way to determine your size is by looking at a pair you already own. Typically, you can find the measurements on the inside of the glasses' arms, indicated by numbers such as 55-17-140. The first number refers to the eye size, the second to the bridge width, and the third to the arm length.
