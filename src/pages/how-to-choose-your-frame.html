Frequently Asked Questions

  • When purchasing a new pair of glasses, the easiest way to determine your size is by looking at a pair you already own. Typically, you can find the measurements on the inside of the glasses' arms, indicated by numbers such as 55-17-140. The first number refers to the eye size, the second to the bridge width, and the third to the arm length.
