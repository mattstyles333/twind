How to choose your glasses

1. Choose your frame

At Spex4Less, we offer a diverse range of frames to suit your personal preferences. Whatever you're looking for, we've got you covered. Our collection includes both affordable stock frames and impressive designer glasses. Find your perfect fit today!

2. Select your lenses

Once you have selected your frame you will have the option to customise your lenses. We have a large selection of different lens coatings for every occasion.

3. Add your prescription

Once you have chosen your frame and lens type you will need to send us your prescription. You can either upload a photo, enter your prescription details manually, load a saved prescription or send your prescription later to info@spex4less.com

4. Order

The final step is to place your order. You can pay with either debit card, credit card or Paypal. Once you have ordered you’ll receive a confirmation email. If you have any further questions regarding your order don't hesitate to get in touch with our customer service team.
