Dispatch & Shipping Information

Dispatch Schedule

We aim to deliver most orders within 1 to 5 working days. However, we ask that you allow up to 15 working days for the delivery of your glasses, as per our terms & conditions. This gives us time to re-do your glasses should there be any quality control issues when processing your order. We will always attempt to notify you immediately of any delays. Delivery Times

The time it takes for your eyewear to arrive depends on a couple of key factors. First, your frame selection: If you opt for our standard stock frames, you can generally expect quicker delivery. On the other hand, if you choose our exclusive designer frames, they might take a bit longer due to their specialty nature. Second, the type of lenses you require plays a role. Our readily available stock lenses ensure faster dispatch, while specialist lenses might add a bit of wait time. Here's a tip: Look out for frames marked with our delivery truck icon, as they are primed for speedier delivery!

Shipping Methods

At Spex4Less we aim to get your order to you as quickly and safely as possible. Working with DPD and Royal Mail, we can provide Tracked delivery options.

You will receive regular email updates about the journey of your parcel from dispatch to your doorstep. You will also be provided with a tracking number so you can track your order whenever you want. On the morning of your delivery day, you will be given an expected delivery time and delivery options where you can change the delivery date or choose a safe place for your parcel can be left if you're not at home. When the delivery has been made, you will receive confirmation together with a photo and GPS coordinates of the delivery location.
