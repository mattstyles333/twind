Delivery Schedule

We aim to deliver most orders within 1 to 7 working days. However, we ask in our terms that you allow up to 15 working days for delivery of your glasses. This allows time for us to re make your glasses should there be any quality failures whilst processing your order. We will always attempt to notify you of any delays. Each pair is individually crafted to your specification and is likely to be dispatched to you as per the schedule below:

  • Standard Frames (Single Vision) Most standard frames with single vision lenses will usually be dispatched between 1 & 7 working days.
  • Standard Frames (Bifocal) Most standard frames with bifocal lenses will usually be dispatched between 2 & 7 working days. Please allow 2 to 3 days longer for anti-reflective coated bifocals.
  • Standard Frames (Varifocal) Varifocal glasses are normally dispatched 5 to 10 working days after we receive your varifocal fitting frame (usually dispatched to you within 1-2 days of receiving your order - unless a designer frame see designer frames below), together with your varifocal photograph and copy of your prescription, back at our glazing lab.
  • Designer Frames Designer frames are usually dispatched within 2 to 10 working days. However, some models & colours may take longer if out of stock with our suppliers. (We will always notify you of any delays with designer frames and give you the option to exchange or cancel)
  • Rimless Frames Rimless glasses are usually dispatched within 2 to 10 working days.
  • Specialist Safety (impact Resistant) Sports Frames & Goggles All specialist safety sports glasses and goggles are usually dispatched within 5 to 10 working days.
  • Sports frames and Sunglasses All sunglasses sports glasses are usually dispatched within 2 to 10 working days. (high wrap and mirrored prescription lenses can take up to 15 working days).
  • Safety Glasses Prescription safety glasses are usually dispatched within 5 to 10 working days.
