Reglaze Glasses

Did you know that your glasses frames can be recycled? With our glasses reglaze service, we can supply lenses made to your new prescription and fit them to your existing frames - a much more cost effective and environmentally-friendly alternative to throwing them away. We can also read the prescription details from your existing lenses to produce a replacement pair if you wish.

Why Use A Reglaze Service?

Our reglaze service is a cost effective method which is perfect for those who want to keep their frames while updating to a new prescription. It's also a great option if your existing lenses are damaged and need replacement. Furthermore, you might consider reglazing to enhance your lenses, opting for our premium lenses like coatings, lens thinning, or varifocals.
