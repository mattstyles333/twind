How To Buy Your Glasses
Navigate the world of online eyewear shopping with our easy step-by-step guide.

Buying your glasses at spex4less

Online shopping has made buying glasses fast and easy. Just follow these 4 simple steps to get the perfect pair delivered to your door.

STEP 1: Choose Your Frame

Your frame should reflect your unique style and personality. At Spex4Less, our vast collection extends from timeless aviators and wayfarers to modern cat-eyes and geometric designs. Beyond shape, selecting the right frame colour can elevate your look, whether you're going for subtle neutrals or bold hues. Moreover, while comfort is essential, it's the precise measurements that make all the difference. Ensuring the glasses fit just right hinges on choosing the perfect dimensions, which will naturally complement the contours of your face. Whether your face is round, square, oval, or heart-shaped, we have the ideal frame size and colour to highlight your best features. Our assortment spans budget-friendly stock frames to luxurious designer selections. Embark on your eyewear journey and discover your ideal match today!

STEP 2: Choose Your Lenses

A frame is not just about style; the lenses you choose play a pivotal role in your comfort and vision clarity. Our extensive lens catalogue ensures that you get the best in class, whether it's polycarbonate, trivex, or high-index lenses. Enhance your user experience by opting for advanced coatings that resist smudge, reduce glare, and protect against UV rays.

STEP 3: Enter Your Prescription

Accuracy is paramount. If you have your prescription handy, please ensure that it's up-to-date. If not, no worries! Place your order and you can send it to us later. Once you have your prescription, forward it to info@spex4less.com. Our team will handle the rest, ensuring your glasses are perfectly tailored to your needs.

STEP 4: Place Your Order

As you finalize your choices and place the order, our team jumps into action. A confirmation email will keep you in the loop, and if there's any need for clarity, our customer service is always at the ready. Your glasses will soon be on their way, crafted with care and precision.

Thank you for choosing Spex4Less. With every pair of glasses, we aim to blend style, comfort, and precision, ensuring that your online shopping experience is nothing short of perfect.
