GOC Claims

At Spex4less we understand the importance of having affordable eyewear available for everyone. There are various different ways in which some people may be able to claim money back on their glasses. Check below to see if your eligible.

Who can claim?

NHS optical voucher scheme

Some people can qualify for an NHS optical voucher depending on their current situation, this can be used to cover the costs of glasses. Check the links below to see if these are applicable to you.

Healthcare cash plans

Health Care plans can also contribute to the cost of your eyewear. Some private healthcare will cover your prescription depending on your health plan with them.

Workplace benefits

Some workplaces offer optical health care cover as a perk. This could be healthcare companies such as Bupa or Simply Health.

Check the forms below and fill them out if they're applicable

Tax Relief

Some tax relief can be applied to eyewear expenses when they are medically necessary.

Disability Benefits

If you are receiving disability benefits you might be eligible to receive help with your glasses costs.

Low income support

These vouchers provide financial support for those with limited income. Check the NHS website for more information to see whether you're eligible.
