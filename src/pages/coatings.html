Lens Coatings

Selecting the perfect frames for your glasses can be a challenge in itself, and then there's the added decision of choosing the right lens coatings. Lens coatings are a special treatment designed to offer extra protection for your lenses. They not only enhance your vision but also elevate the overall quality of your new glasses. These coatings work wonders by boosting durability, improving performance, and enhancing the aesthetics of all lens types.

The coating you ultimately pick should align with your unique visual requirements and lifestyle. Delve into our informative guide to discover the distinct benefits of each lens coating, empowering you to make the ideal choice that perfectly suits your needs.

UV and Durable+ Coating

Ultraviolet protecting coatings can be applied to clear glasses as well as sunglasses. This is especially important for people who spend a lot of time outside. Even on cloudy days, the UV risk can still be high. A UV coating will block harmful rays and keep your eyes protected against potential damage.

Anti-Reflective Coating

Eliminating lens reflections allows more light in, improving vision and decreasing eye strain. This also gives glasses a sleek look. The coating is particularly beneficial for drivers, improving safety by quickly identifying road hazards. Nighttime glare from lights is also reduced, ensuring a comfortable drive.

Blue Light Coating

Blue light from devices and daylight has a short wavelength that penetrates eyes, risking retinal damage and strain. A blue light filter in glasses neutralizes this, protecting your eyes and providing a more comfortable visual experience.

Anti-Fog Coating

Our anti-fog coating effectively absorbs moisture to combat lens fog-ups. It's especially beneficial when wearing masks, enjoying hot drinks, cooking, or being outdoors. Experience consistently clear visibility, even in challenging situations.

Honeycomb Coating

Eliminating lens reflections allows more light in, improving vision and decreasing eye strain. This also gives glasses a sleek look. The coating is particularly beneficial for drivers, improving safety by quickly identifying road hazards. Nighttime glare from lights is also reduced, ensuring a comfortable drive.
