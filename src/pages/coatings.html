Lens Coatings

Selecting the perfect frames for your glasses can be a challenge in itself, and then there's the added decision of choosing the right lens coatings. Lens coatings are a special treatment designed to offer extra protection for your lenses, not only enhancing your vision but also elevating the overall quality of your new glasses.

UV and Durable+ Coating

Ultraviolet protection coatings can be applied to clear glasses as well as sunglasses. This is especially important for people who spend a lot of time outside. Even on cloudy days, the UV risk can still be considerably high. Our UV coating will block harmful rays and keep your eyes protected against UV related damage.

Anti-Reflective Coating

Eliminating lens reflections allows more light in, improving vision and decreasing eye strain. This also gives glasses a sleek look. The coating is particularly beneficial for drivers, improving safety by quickly identifying road hazards. Night-time glare from lights is also reduced, ensuring a comfortable drive.

Blue Light Coating

Blue light from devices and daylight has a short wavelength that penetrates eyes, risking retinal damage and strain. A blue light filter in glasses neutralizes this, protecting your eyes and providing a more comfortable visual experience.

Hydrophobic Coating

Our hydrophobic coating effectively absorbs moisture to combat lens fog-ups. It's especially beneficial when wearing masks, enjoying hot drinks, cooking, or being outdoors. Experience consistently clear visibility, even in challenging situations.
