Who We Are

Established in 2004, Spex4less is one of the first online glasses companies in the industry and provides prescription glasses, sunglasses and sports glasses at a fraction of high-street prices. With deep roots within optics we have successfully mastered the supply of high-quality, yet affordable spectacles via the internet, with the highest standard of care and service.

Based in Hoylake, Wirral, United Kingdom, our's is a small family-run business whose ethos is focused on ensuring that every customer receives only the best from us. We pride ourselves on our amazing "Excellent" Trustpilot rating from over 25,000 reviews. Our business comprises a huge amount of repeat customers, which speaks volumes about the story of our success.
