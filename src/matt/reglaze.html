The Industry's Best-Kept Secret

Reglazing is the optical industry's hidden gem that most retailers don't want you to know about.

What is Reglazing?

Reglazing is the professional process of fitting new prescription lenses into your existing frames. It's like giving your favorite glasses a new lease of life, without the premium price tag of new frames.

The Simple Process

  1. 1 Send us your frames and prescription
  2. 2 We professionally remove your old lenses
  3. 3 Cut and fit new lenses to your exact prescription
  4. 4 Clean, polish, and return your upgraded glasses

Why The Industry Keeps This Quiet

Major retailers make significant profits by selling you new frames with every prescription change. They rarely mention that you can keep your existing frames and just replace the lenses ‚Äì often at a fraction of the cost.
