Glasses Care and Maintenance

To keep your glasses in top condition and ensure they provide you with clear vision for as long as possible, proper care and maintenance are crucial. Here are some tips to help you take care of your eyewear:

  • Use a soft cloth and mild soap to clean your glasses regularly.
  • Steer clear of harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that can scratch or damage the lenses.
  • Use a microfiber cloth to gently clean the frames and lenses without causing any scratches.
  • Store your glasses in a protective case when you're not wearing them to prevent accidental damage.
  • Avoid exposing your glasses to extreme temperatures or direct sunlight.
