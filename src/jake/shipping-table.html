Production Times

The delivery time for your eyewear depends on a few key factors. If you select a standard stock frame, you can generally expect quicker delivery, while exclusive designer frames may take a bit longer due to their specialty nature. Similarly, choosing standard lenses usually results in faster dispatch, whereas specialist lenses may add a slight delay.

Shipping Methods

At Spex4Less, we are dedicated to delivering your orders swiftly and securely through our partnership with Royal Mail. Throughout the shipping process, you will receive frequent email updates, a tracking number, and the flexibility to adjust delivery dates or specify a safe location for your parcel's drop-off if you're not at home. On the day of delivery, you'll receive information about the expected delivery time. Once your order is successfully delivered, we will promptly confirm its completion, accompanied by a photo and GPS coordinates of the delivery location, ensuring a seamless and transparent delivery experience for you.
