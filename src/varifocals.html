What are varifocal glasses?

Discover the perfect solution for clear vision at any distance with our Varifocal Glasses. These innovative lenses seamlessly merge reading, distance, and intermediate prescriptions into a single lens, eliminating the need for multiple pairs of glasses.

Here's how they work: Unlike traditional glasses with distinct zones for different distances, Varifocals offer a smooth transition between these zones. This means you can effortlessly shift your gaze from reading a book to focusing on something in the distance without the need to swap eyewear.
