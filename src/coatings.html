Anti-Reflective Coating

This coating eliminates surface reflections on a lens, enabling more light transmission, enhancing vision, reducing eye strain, and improving the aesthetics of glasses. An anti-reflective coat also enhances driver safety by minimizing glare from streetlights or headlights, leading to faster recognition of road hazards, especially at night.

Blue-light Coating

Blue light exists in both natural daylight and electronic devices like computers, smartphones, TVs, and strip lights. With its short wavelength, blue light easily enters the eyes, leading to potential issues like retinal damage, eye strain, and fatigue.

UV and Durable+ Coating

Our UV and Durable+ coating offers a combination of UV protection and scratch resistance. Harmful UV rays from sunlight are countered by this coating, enhancing eye safety. Moreover, it includes a layer that resists scratches, maintaining the lens quality amid everyday use.
