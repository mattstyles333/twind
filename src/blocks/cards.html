Varifocal Glasses

This coating eliminates surface reflections on a lens, enabling more light transmission, enhancing vision, reducing eye strain, and improving the aesthetics of glasses. An anti-reflective coat also enhances driver safety by minimizing glare from streetlights or headlights, leading to faster recognition of road hazards, especially at night.
