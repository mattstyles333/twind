Production Times

The time it takes for your eyewear to arrive depends on a couple of key factors. First, your frame selection: If you opt for our standard stock frames, you can generally expect quicker delivery. On the other hand, if you choose our exclusive designer frames, they might take a bit longer due to their specialty nature. Second, the type of lenses you require plays a role. Our readily available standard lenses ensure faster dispatch, while specialist lenses might add a bit of wait time. Here's a tip: Look out for frames marked with our delivery truck icon, as they are primed for speedier delivery!

Stock Frames

Our stock frames are readily available and can generally be delivered quickly. For more information and to view our complete collection, check out Our Stock Frames.

Non-Prescription

When ordering an ophthalmic frame, the frame will arrive with the original clear dummy lenses. If you order a non-prescription sunglass frame, the frame will come with the original non-prescription sunglass lenses as pictured.

Standard Lenses

Our standard lenses include low-index single vision prescription lenses, providing you with reliable and affordable vision correction.

Specialist Lenses

Our specialist lenses include high index, high prescription, wrapped, multi-focal, mirrored, polarised, and transition lenses, offering advanced options for various vision needs.
