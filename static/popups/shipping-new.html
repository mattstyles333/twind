Production Times

The time it takes for your eyewear to arrive depends on a couple of key factors. First, your frame selection: If you opt for our standard stock frames, you can generally expect quicker delivery. On the other hand, if you choose our exclusive designer frames, they might take a bit longer due to their specialty nature. Second, the type of lenses you require plays a role. Our readily available standard lenses ensure faster dispatch, while specialist lenses might add a bit of wait time. Here's a tip: Look out for frames marked with our delivery truck icon, as they are primed for speedier delivery!
