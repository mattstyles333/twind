Processing

  1. Prescription Review: Immediately upon receipt, each prescription is checked for errors, including typos and inaccuracies in pupillary distance.
  2. Frame Selection: We select frames that exactly match the specifications in model, size, and colour as stated in the order.
  3. Lens Order: Lenses are ordered based on the customer's prescription, chosen coatings, and necessary specifications.
  4. Lens Inspection: Upon arrival, each lens is inspected to ensure it matches the prescription and is free of defects from the supplier.
  5. Preparation for Lab: Once verified, prescriptions and frames are prepared and sent to our lab for assembly.

Lab

  1. Frame and Colour Check: Before assembly, we ensure the frame model and colour are exactly as ordered.
  2. Lens Fitting Test: Using a lens focimeter, we verify that the prescription is accurate.
  3. Alignment Verification: The alignment of the frame and nose pads is checked to confirm they are correctly positioned.
  4. Imperfection Inspection: We inspect each frame and lens for scratches, chips, or any other imperfections.
  5. Final Assembly Check: After assembly, glasses undergo a final focimeter test to confirm all specifications are met.

Dispatch

  1. Final Review: Before dispatch, we conduct a final review to ensure the frame colour, size, and model are as per the order.
  2. Lens Coating Check: We inspect the lenses for the correct application of coatings such as photochromicity, polarisation, and anti-reflective properties.
  3. Case and Packaging Inspection: The quality of the case and overall packaging is assessed for any defects and to ensure adequate protection.
  4. Blemish Check: We check for blemishes or imperfections on the glasses before they are packaged.
  5. Address Verification: The shipping address is verified for accuracy to ensure the glasses are delivered to the correct location.
