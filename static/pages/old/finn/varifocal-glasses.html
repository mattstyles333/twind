What Are Varifocal Glasses?

A varifocal lens - often referred to as progressive glasses - is a marvel of modern optics, offering multiple focal points all in the same lens design. With just one pair of glasses, varifocal lenses are able to seamlessly merge a reading prescription, a distance prescription, and intermediate vision clarity all into one lens. How Do Varifocal Lenses Work?

Put simply, varifocal lenses work by layering different prescriptions in a methodic way. The top part of the varifocal lens is for distance vision, the middle is for intermediate distances like your computer screen, and the bottom is for close-up tasks like reading. The transition between these zones is smooth and gradual, providing a natural viewing experience. Benefits of Varifocal Glasses vs. Single Vision Lenses

Varifocal lenses offer a significant advantage over single-vision lenses by offering various focal points, as opposed to singles which correct for only one distance. With varifocals, you eliminate the hassle of constantly switching between different pairs of glasses. Plus, they provide a seamless transition between near, intermediate, and distance vision, enhancing your overall visual comfort and convenience.

Customisation Options for Your Varifocal Glasses

Varifocal lenses can be tailored to your individual needs. You can choose from different lens designs that prioritise specific visual zones based on your lifestyle. Additionally, you can add coatings to enhance clarity, reduce glare, and protect your lenses from scratches.

Why Frame Depth Matters for Varifocals

Choosing the right frame depth is crucial for getting the most out of your varifocal lenses. While more expensive varifocal lenses are often marketed as superior, a deeper frame can actually provide a better visual experience compared to a shallower frame. Frames with a depth greater than 30mm give more room for the different focal zones to work effectively, providing a smoother and more comfortable transition between distance, intermediate, and near vision.
